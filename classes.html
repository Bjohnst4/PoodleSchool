Classes Available:

Behavior & Equiqette classes

  • This is an entry level course required for all security poodles in training. Poodles enrolled in Behavior and Etiquette will learn the most basic commands such as sit, fetch, attack, watch, heel, and many many more.
  • Prerecquisite Courses: N/A

Advanced Surveillance

  • Poodles enrolled in this intermediate level course will learn more advanced surveillance techniques such as prolonged surveillance up to six hours, what kind of behavior to react to and what to ignore, and how to react in case of an emergency.
  • Prerecquisite Courses: Behavior & Etiquette

Bark Control

  • Poodles enrolled in Bark Control will learn about acceptable times to use barks and how to use them most affectively.
  • Prerecquisite Courses: Behavior & Etiquette

Attack Methods

  • Attack Methods is the final and most advanced poodle protection training course. Protection Poodles who have completed this course will be well trained in multiple attack methods such as bite, chase, tackle, and disarm. In addition, poodles will be aware of scenarios in which case each attack method is most effective.
  • Prerecquisite Courses: Behavior and Etiquette, Advanced Surveillance, & Bark Control
